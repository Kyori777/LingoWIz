Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some questions that are frequently asked in our website.

Is LingoWiz free?

Of course! we created this website primarily for students without expecting anything in return, but if we are able to improve upon its unique feature, a small charge might apply.

What is the function of the Text Summarizer?

The function of a Text Summarizer is to generate a concise and coherent summary of a lengthy text document. It aims to capture the main points and essential information from the original text, presenting it in a shorter and more manageable form while retaining its accuracy and coherence.

What is the function of the Grammar Checker?

The Grammar Checker is an essential tool designed to assist in identifying and rectifying grammatical errors within your writing. Additionally, it can help recognize stylistic errors and offer suggestions for enhancing the quality of your content.

What is the Paraphraser tool?

The Paraphraser tool is a valuable resource that can assist in the process of rephrasing or rewording existing content to create multiple unique versions. This tool is useful for individuals seeking to produce diverse variations of a given text.
